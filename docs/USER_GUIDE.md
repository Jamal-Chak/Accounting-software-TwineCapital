## Dashboard
The command center of your business.
-   **Widgets**: View real-time cash flow, pending invoices, and expense summaries.
-   **Quick Actions**: Create invoices or bills directly from the dashboard card.

## Banking & Reconciliation
Manage your cash and bank connections.
-   **Connect Bank**: Use the "Connect Bank" button to securely link your accounts via our provider.
-   **Transactions**: View all imported transactions.
-   **Reconciliation**: Go to **Banking > Reconciliation** to match bank feed transactions with your ledger. The AI will suggest matches based on amount and date.
-   **Autobookkeeping**: The system learns from your categorization to automate future entries.

## Sales
Manage revenue and customers.
-   **Estimates**: Create professional quotes. Convert them to invoices with one click.
-   **Invoices**: Generate tax-compliant invoices. Send them via email or WhatsApp.
-   **Customers**: Maintain a database of your clients with payment history.

## Purchases
Track spending and suppliers.
-   **Expenses**: Log out-of-pocket expenses. Snap a photo of receipts for auto-creation.
-   **Bills**: Track accounts payable (supplier invoices) to ensure on-time payment.
-   **Suppliers**: Manage vendor details and default expense categories.

## Time Tracking
For service-based businesses.
-   **Projects**: Create projects to track profitability.
-   **Timesheets**: Log hours against projects. These can be billed directly to clients.

## Analytics
Data-driven insights.
-   **Risk Console**: Detect potential fraud or duplicate payments.
-   **Health Score**: A composite score (0-100) of your business financial health.
-   **Reports**: Access standard financial reports (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow).
